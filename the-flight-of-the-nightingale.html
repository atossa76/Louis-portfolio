The Flight of the Nightingale - 2023

Flight of the Nightingale is a anti-war short film that challenges the notion that the victors are the ones who tell the history. This film makes use of archive footage of the allied bombing campaign, with an electronic soundtrack that includes a field recording from 1942 that captures a nightingales song disrupted by hundreds of Lancaster bombers. The nightingale has a strong symbolic link to Ukrainian literature, poetry and music.
