Last Word FM - 2023

For the Roundhouse’s Last Word Poetry Festival, three short podcasts were recorded and produced, delving into the festival’s history and celebrating its legacy. The podcasts featured interviews with poets who have contributed to the program over the years, offering a behind-the-scenes look at their creative journeys. A key highlight was a live interview with renowned poet Ellen Renton, capturing the dynamic spirit of the festival and its impact on the poetry community.
