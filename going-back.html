Going Back - 2024

‘Going Back’ is a podcast commissioned by Roundhouse Transmission as part of their Audio Canvas Award. The series follows a heartfelt journey to the ancestral homes of the creator’s grandmothers, nestled in a hilltop village in rural Italy and the picturesque Harz Mountains of Germany. Through interviews with his parents, the podcast uncovers vivid childhood memories of these places, weaving together personal narratives and cultural history. Going Back serves as a call to action for young people to explore their heritage and preserve their ancestral stories in digital form, ensuring these histories endure for future generations.
