Graffham Bell-ringers - 2024

Graffham Bell-ringers is a visual documentary that follows George, a dedicated bell ringer who has practiced his craft in the village of Graffham for over 40 years. Capturing a day in his life, the film uses layered field recordings—from distant hills, throughout the village, and inside the bell tower—to showcase the rich, resonant sounds of the bells. It reflects on the fading tradition of bell ringing as younger generations grow less interested, while highlighting its importance in creating a shared sonic identity and preserving cultural heritage within the community.
