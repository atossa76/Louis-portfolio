Into the Light - 2021

Into the Light is a visual documentary directed by Moses Fiddian-Green that intimately follows a block of Carrara marble from deep within the quarry to the workshop of renowned sculptor Nic Fiddian-Green, known for his iconic horse head sculptures. The film captures the raw beauty of the creative process, blending stunning visuals with a rich, immersive soundtrack that incorporates on-location field recordings and an evocative electronic music score composed by Louis Higgins. Celebrated for its artistry, Into the Light has garnered numerous awards and was officially selected for the Rome Independent Film Festival.
